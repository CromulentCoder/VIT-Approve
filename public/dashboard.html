FAQs

  • What Is This Site For?

    The purpose of this site is to provide a platform for the students already living in the hostels to rate the services including their blocks and hostels.

  • Why Rate Hostels and Mess?

    The main reason to do so is to improve the living and make the authorities aware of the problems students face through an interactive manner.

    Also this site aims to serve as a document for the new students who are in their 1st year of college or those who may wish to join it after their schooling

  • What do the graphs indicate?

    The graphs are used to show in an interactive manner the opinions of the students collected through their form responses

    Each graph shows the Total Average for the particular response.
